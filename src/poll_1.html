The Agnipath scheme was approved by the Indian Government in June 2022 to be implemented from September 2022. The announcement was done on June 14, 2022.[10] The scheme is for both male and female aspirants of age group 17.5 to 21 years. In the midst of widespread protests against the Agnipath scheme, the Central Government raised the upper limit from 21 to 23, but only for recruitment in the year 2022.[11][12] The recruitment through this scheme is twice a year for the Indian Army, the Indian Navy and the Indian Air Force.
